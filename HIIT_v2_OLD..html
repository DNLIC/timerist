How to Use This HIIT Timer

  • Configure: Tap Set Up to choose work/rest times, rounds, total sets, warmup, cooldown, and more.
  • Start: After setup, tap the big Start button to begin the countdown.
  • Controls during workout: Pause to rest, Reset/Clear to start over.
  • Sound & cues: Enable sound for beeps and voice announcements (important on iOS — tap "Enable Sound" first).
  • Pro tip: Use full-screen mode in the gym for distraction-free timing.
