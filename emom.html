About This Timer

EMOM (Every Minute On the Minute) timer for structured workouts. Each round is exactly 60 seconds. When the countdown reaches 0, a beep sounds and the timer immediately resets to 60 seconds for the next round.

Keywords: EMOM timer, every minute on the minute, EMOM workout, interval timer, fitness timer, workout timer, exercise timer, HIIT timer, time-based training.
